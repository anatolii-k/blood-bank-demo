Blood Typing and Transfusion Compatibility

Blood transfusions require matching both the ABO group and Rh factor. Below is a compatibility table:

Recipient Blood Type	Matching Donor Blood Type
A+			A+, A-, O+, O-
A-			A-, O-
B+			B+, B-, O+, O-
B-			B-, O-
AB+			Compatible with all blood types
AB-			AB-, A-, B-, O-
O+			O+, O-
O-			O-
